=== HOW TO USE THESE TEMPLATES ===
1. Log in to your EmailJS account
2. Go to Email Templates
3. Create two new templates:
   a. Admin Notification Template
   b. User Confirmation Template
4. Copy and paste the respective template content
5. Save the templates
6. Note down the Template IDs for both templates
7. Replace 'YOUR_TEMPLATE_ID' in the JavaScript code with these IDs

=== TEMPLATE CUSTOMIZATION TIPS ===
1. You can add your logo to the templates
2. Customize colors and styling using HTML/CSS
3. Add social media links
4. Include your business hours or response time expectations
5. Add a footer with legal information if needed

=== SECURITY NOTES ===
